The Last School of Design is a team of mentors and coaches, all experts in their field (UX Design, Video design, product design, Marketing, accountancy and legal councel) who help creative people and entrepreneurs develop their personal projects. LSD is based in Brussels, Belgium.

As a student, your vision and work force will define your own personal curriculum. Whether you need coaching, training or support, top experts will be by your side. We'll help you make your project happen. A personal project is thrilling but also scary and lonely at times. With LSD, you are not alone.

You on the other hand, provide the motivation, enthusiasm, vision and work force (we will not do the actual production work). This ensures that your project remains entirely yours.

The Last School of Design welcomes your projects in a not-so-distant future. Join our twitter feed to stay in touch.
